Dishes Made By Our Professionals



Kulfi Falooda

This refreshing treat combines thin falooda noodles and kulfi made with slowly simmered whole milk that is flavored with pistachio, rose water, and saffron.

Sabudana Vada

These crispy Indian fritters consist of a combination of soaked tapioca pearls, mashed potatoes, roasted and crushed peanuts, and a variety of spices.

Panner Tikka

This North Indian tikka variety consists of a combination of marinated paneer cheese and vegetables that are cooked in a tandoor - a traditional Indian oven.

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa made from a batter of soaked rice and lentils that is baked into a thin pancake and stuffed with potatoes, onions, and mustard seeds.

Uttapam

Visually similar to a pizza, uttapam is a South Indian dish consisting of a batter that is fried and topped with chopped vegetables such as onions and tomatoes

Mysore Pak

As the King Krishna Raja Wodeyar was ready to have his lunch, the chef began to make a sweet dish, combining gram flour, ghee butter, and sugar into a syrup.

Aloo Paratha

Aloo paratha is one of the most popular breakfast foods in northern India, a flatbread stuffed with a spicy potato mixture.

Shrikhand

Shrikhand is a popular Indian yogurt-based dessert, combined with sugar and fruits in order to develop a rich, creamy texture and sweet flavor.

Dhokla

Dhokla is a vegetarian snack consisting of fermented chickpea batter and rice. Spices such as chili and ginger are added in order to improve the flavor of the dish.

Gajar Ka Halwa

Gajar ka halwa is a sweet pudding made with grated carrots, milk, sugar, nuts, clarified butter known as ghee, and dried milk known as khoya.

Bhelpuri

There is a lot of debate about what should go in a bhelpuri, but the most commonly used ingredients include puffed rice, potatoes, fried noodles, onions, and chilis.

Kachori

Kachori is a spicy Indian snack shaped like a round flattened ball, consisting of a flour-based shell with different fillings.

Chole Bhature

chole bhature is a combination of two dishes: chole - a spicy chickpea curry, and bhature - a type of fried bread made with maida flour.

Misal Pav

Its two main components are misal – a curry made with sprouted moth beans – and pav, bread rolls used to mop up the flavorful curry.

Raita

Raita is a popular Indian side dish made by combining thick yogurt with different fruits, vegetables, and spices, so it can be either sweet or savory and spicy.

Pakora

Pakora is a savory, deep-fried Indian snack made with pieces of vegetables such as cauliflower and eggplant.

Ras Malai

Ras malai Indian dessert consisting of white cream, sugar, milk, and cardamom-flavored paneer cheese. Almonds, cashews, and saffron are often added to the dessert.

Dal Makhani

It consists of red kidney beans and whole black lentils.The dish is prepared with hefty amounts of ghee and various seasonings such as ginger garlic paste.

Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is a popular street snack originating from Maharashtra. It consists of a vegetable curry that is typically served with a soft bread roll known as pav.

Palak Paneer

Palak paneer is an Indian vegetarian dish made with paneer cheese in a thick sauce consisting of puréed spinach mixed with tomatoes, garam masala and garlic.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun is a dessert based on milk solids that are kneaded into a dough, shaped into balls, and fried in ghee. The balls then get soaked in a sugary syrup.

Vada Pav

Its name referring to the key ingredients: vada, or spicy mashed potatoes that are deep-fried in chickpea batter, and pav, or white bread rolls.

Khichdi

Khichdi is a South Asian dish consisting of rice and lentils, its name derived from the Sanskrit khiccā, denoting a dish of rice and legumes

Pani-Puri

Small in size, it consists of a hollow puri that is fried until crispy, then stuffed with a combination of flavored water called pani and tamarind chutney
